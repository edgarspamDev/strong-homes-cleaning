# Feature Roadmap

## Tier 1: Launch Critical (The "Must Haves")
- [ ] **Silent Video Hero:** Cross-browser compatible loop.
- [ ] **Sticky Mobile CTA:** "Call Now" or "Get Quote" always visible.
- [ ] **Service Area Gating:** Simple JS check for NWI cities.
- [ ] **Lead Capture Form:** Sends email notification to owner.
- [ ] **City Pages:** Programmatic generation of 13 starter cities.

## Tier 2: Strong Recommendations (Conversion Boosters)
- [ ] **"What's Included" Checklist:** Visual comparison of Standard vs. Deep.
- [ ] **SMS Auto-Reply (Mockup):** System to text leads immediately (requires API).
- [ ] **Review Widget:** Placeholders for now, swap with Google Reviews Embed later.
- [ ] **Before/After Slider:** *Only if real photos provided.*

## Tier 3: Advanced (Future State)
- [ ] **Stripe Deposit:** $50 hold to book date.
- [ ] **Real-time Availability:** Integration with Calendly or specialized cleaning CRM.
- [ ] **Customer Portal:** Login to view past invoices.
